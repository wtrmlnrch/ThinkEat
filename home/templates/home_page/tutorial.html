How to Use ThinkEat

  1. Sign Up: ThinkEat requires an account, so click on my account and navigate to register so you can sign up today! If you already have an account, simply log in.
  2. Navigating the Website: When using the navbars click any text to navigate to that page.
    - Tutorial: Click here to learn how to use ThinkEat
    - About: Click here to learn more about ThinkEat and the minds behind it
    - Icon: Click on Braxton our mascot, to return to the home Page
    - ThinkEat: Click here to utilize ThinkEat's main functionality
    - My Account: Click here to access your account and view your saved recipes
  3. Select a Mode: Use the dropdown menu to choose between Ingredients Mode and Dish Name Mode depending on your specific needs.
  4. Enter Your Input:
    - Using the Ingredients Mode, you should type a comma-separated list like this: "eggs, spinach, cheese". That will give you a couple of options of recipes to pick from
    - Using the Dish Name Mode, you simply need to type the dish name you want like this: "Chicken Parmesan" This will give you instructions to cook sed recipe with the ingredients needed.
  5. Explore Recipes:
    In Ingredients Mode, ThinkEat will show you up to 5 recipe suggestions at a time. Type the number or name of a recipe to get detailed instructions, or click the More button for additional suggestions!
  6. Reset When Needed:
    Click the Reset button to clear your current session with Braxton and start fresh!
  7. Switch Modes Anytime:
    Use the mode dropdown to switch between inputs when needed. Be Aware this will clear you current log, so make sure you save your current recipe if needed!
  8. Save Your Recipes:
    Use the "save recipe" button and have your current recipe stored within your account information!
  9. View Your Saved Recipes:
    Click on the my account button and click on the saved recipes button. That will bring you to a page of all of your favorites!
  10. My Account:
    When viewing your account information, you can change any of your information! Simply, change what you want, then click the "update info" button to save it. Be Aware you cannot change your username to that of another existing user.
